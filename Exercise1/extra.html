In this extra exercise I simply want to render the "Burning Ship" Mandelbrot set on the canvas.

In the middle of this page, you can see two canvases. The left one will be filled with a rendering of the so-called "Burning Ship" Mandelbrot set.

The burning shipis given by the following formula \begin{align} z_{k+1} &= F(z_k) + z_0, k = 1, 2, ... \\ &where \\ F(z_n) &= (|Re(z)+i*Im(z)|)^2 \end{align}
